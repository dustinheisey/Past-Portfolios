Frequently Asked Questions

What is your experience in web design?

With over 3 years of experience in web design and development, I have created numerous websites for businesses and non-profit organizations across different industries.

What is your approach to web design?

At the heart of my web design approach is your success. I collaborate closely with you to develop a custom strategy and design that aligns with your vision and exceeds your expectations.

How do you ensure that the website aligns with the overall brand and marketing strategy?

I work closely with my clients to ensure a consistent brand message. This includes understanding the brand's messaging and voice, as well as ensuring that the website's design, content, and user experience are consistent with the brand's values and goals.

How do you optimize the website for user experience?

I prioritize user experience in all aspects of web design, from the layout and navigation to the content and functionality. I strive to ensure that the website is intuitive, easy to use, and meets the needs of its target audience.

How can you ensure a website is accessible?

I ensure that the website adheres to the latest accessibility standards and guidelines, including providing alternative text for images, using proper heading structures, designing a AA contrast compliant color palette, and ensuring that the website is compatible with screen readers.

How can you make a website environmentally sustainable?

I am committed to making websites environmentally sustainable by minimizing the website's carbon footprint. This includes optimizing website code, using energy-efficient hosting, and implementing sustainable design practices. I strive to create websites that contribute to a healthier planet.
