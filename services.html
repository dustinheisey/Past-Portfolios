My Process

I prioritize designing websites that are both user-friendly and eco-friendly. My four-step process ensures that your website meets accessibility standards, is optimized for performance, and has a lower carbon footprint than most sites.

Discover

I will work to understand your business goals, target audience, and their accessibility needs. I will research accessibility barriers and sustainable requirements to ensure a user-centric design.

Design & Develop

I will design and develop a website that's accessible, sustainable, visually appealing, and fast-loading. I will use sustainable development practices to optimize code, media, and web elements.

Debug

I will test website accessibility, performance, and carbon footprint to ensure compliance with standards. I will conduct quality assurance checks to fix issues.

Deliver

I will launch your website and can maintain it through regular audits, updates, and adherence to accessibility and sustainability standards. I can keep your website user-friendly and eco-friendly for years to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the timeline for custom web design and development?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project, but I aim to complete it in 1 to 2 weeks. I work closely with you to establish a timeline that fits your needs and provide regular updates throughout the process.

How do you communicate with clients?

I prefer text-based methods like email, chat, or direct text. This allows for clear and concise responses and a written record of our conversations. I'm still willing to hop on a call if necessary, but I believe clear and direct communication is crucial for a successful project.

What is a design system and why is it important?

A design system is a set of guidelines that ensure a consistent look and feel across all pages of your website. It saves time, resources, and money while improving brand recognition and creating a better user experience.

Why is website accessibility important?

Website accessibility provides equal access to information and services for all users. It increases your reach, improves user experience, and demonstrates social responsibility. Additionally, it can help avoid legal issues and improve search engine rankings.

Why does website sustainability matter?

A sustainable website reduces its carbon footprint, lowers costs, and promotes social responsibility, which can positively impact brand image and attract and retain customers.

Why is website maintenance important?

Website maintenance ensures your website is up-to-date, secure, and performing well. It also prevents downtime, data loss, and potential security breaches.
